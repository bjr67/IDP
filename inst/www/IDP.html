Calculation of Rh of IDPs



Enter a sequence of the one-letter abbreviation of the residues of an intrinisically disordered protein (IDP) and this tool will calculate the length, net positive charge, net negative charge, net charge, polyproline-II helix propensities, and the hydrodynamic radius. Propensities for polyproline-II helical confirmation were used from the Hisler Data set.
